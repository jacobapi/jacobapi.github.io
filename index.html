Jacob Lin

I am an Assistant Professor at NTU in the Department of Civil Engineering. Earlier, I received my PhD in CE and MS in CS from UIUC advised by Mani Golparvar-Fard. We started an AI construction company Reconstruct with Derek Hoiem and Joe Degol.

My research focus on developing and validating computer vision and machine learning algorithms with visual data and Building Information Modelling (BIM) for construction project controls.
